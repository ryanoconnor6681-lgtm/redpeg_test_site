Why RedPeg

YOUR AI IS POWERFUL. THE LAST MILE HASN'T CHANGED.

Your tools can generate the concept in an afternoon. Your internal team can write the brief. Consultancies can build the strategy deck. The front end of experiential marketing has been transformed.

The back end is still ours. Someone has to translate that render into engineering drawings. Negotiate the venue. Manage the fabrication. Hire and train a 200-person crew. Secure permits in three jurisdictions. And make sure the experience is flawless when 80,000 people walk through the door.

The tools that get you to the brief got better. The team that takes you from brief to reality has been doing this for 30 years. We're that team.

We're fluent in AI and creative strategy, too. When you need help upstream, we're there. But our product is the actualization, the last mile between vision and reality.

Large-Scale Events

What we deliver

CAD translation, fabrication management, venue procurement and permitting, vendor packages, staffing and labor, logistics and shipping, load-in/load-out, on-site production management, and post-event reporting.

02

Employee Experiences

What you bring

Curriculum, content, speakers, brand themes, attendance numbers, objectives. The strategic framework is yours. The experience design, the build, and the execution are ours.

What we deliver

Environment design and fabrication, AV and technology, setup and teardown (10-12 cohorts for one client's leadership program), digital experience builds, staffing, venue management, registration systems, on-site production.

Music Partnerships
